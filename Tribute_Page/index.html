Bob Saget: A Family Man with a Dark Twist

Bob's Highlights

Professional

  • From a young age, Bob developed his sense of humor in school
  • Instead of becoming a doctor, one of his teachers influenced him to pursue a career in film
  • Bob appeared in 18 movies and over 40 TV shows
  • You may know Bob as an actor, but he also had a knack for stand up comedy

Personal

  • Born to a Jewish household in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, on May 17, 1956
  • High Schools: Lake Taylor High, Abington Senior High School
  • College: Temple University to major in Bachelor of Arts.
  • Graduate School: a few days at Universtiy of Southern California
  • Married in 1982 to Sheri Kramer and had 3 daughters

Summary

The best way to describe Bob is passionate. Whatever he did, he sunk his heart into to it and ended up impacting those around him. Whether you were watching him on TV or listening to him in a podcast, he had clearly mastered the art of communication.

During his comedy routines, he had no problem speaking what was on his mind. Many of his jokes come from real world problems and things he'd faced in his lifetime. Given his Jewish background, many of the jokes made light of his childhood and how the traditions of the religion affected him.

Bob was mostly known for his role in Full House and America's Funniest Home Videos. As a widowed dad in Full House, the audience grew to know him as a family guy and connected so well with him that when he came back for Fuller House, it was almost as if he never left.

Bob's Famous Shows

Dumb and Dumberer: When Harry Met Lloyd

Dumb and Dumberer: When Harry Met Lloyd is the 2nd movie in the Dumb and Dumber movies that acted as a prequel, showing how Harry met Lloyd in the first place. Bob played Walter Matthews in the movie.

Madagascar

Madagascar is about a group of zoo animals escaping the zoo, just to find themselves stranded on the island of Madagascar. Bob plays a small role as the Zoo animal in the movie.

Full House

Full House is a tv series about a widowed dad and several family members occupying a huge house in San Francisco. Bob plays Danny Turner in the show.

Fuller House

Fuller House is the sequel series to Full House in which nearly 20 years go by and many of the family and friends end up having their own family. Bob plays an older Danny Turner in the series.

The Masked Singer

The Masked Singer is a reality tv show where different contestants, ranging from Grammy award winners to professional video gamers to Youtube influencers, sing a song in a mask to compete against the other contestants. Bob sang as the Squiggly Monster, a contestant in the show.
